# Research Roadmap

1. EXP-001: local Qiskit/Aer circuit and measurement infrastructure, including computational-basis states, X/H/Z gates, superposition, repeated sampling, and Bell-state entanglement.
2. EXP-002: complete Max-Cut reference problem checked against brute force, verified QUBO/Ising algebra, and genuine local Qiskit QAOA.
3. EXP-003: deterministic synthetic tune families.
4. EXP-004: a transparent two-family QUBO for graph partitioning.
5. EXP-005A: complete genuine local Qiskit p=1 QAOA for the registered tune-family QUBO, clearly separated from the current classical fallback sampler.
6. EXP-006: local noise sensitivity studies.
7. EXP-007: optional IBM hardware dry-runs and explicit QPU submission safeguards.
8. Later work: bounded public symbolic-music experiments after licence, provenance, privacy, and cultural-context review.

EXP-001 and EXP-002 are local-only and require no IBM account. They should be understood as bounded ideal-simulation reference experiments, not evidence of quantum advantage.
