# Known limitations

- Exhaustive exact enumeration is authoritative for this eight-variable experiment.
- The fixture is deterministic and synthetic; it is not real folk-family discovery or culturally
  authentic evidence.
- No quantum advantage, speedup, scalability, or production claim is supported.
- Qiskit uses optional local ideal simulation only; there is no hardware execution.
- The OpenAI SDK and Qiskit stack are optional dependencies.
- The product deliberately offers one fixed guided journey, not arbitrary tune uploads.
- Finite-shot sampling is uncertain; a best sampled optimum does not prove an optimum expectation.
- A partition and its global bitwise complement are the same unlabeled family split.
- GPT-5.6 explanations are optional and may fall back when unavailable or invalid.
